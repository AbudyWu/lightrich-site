公司簡介 About

銧豐國際成立於 2014 年，提供 EMS 代工服務，支援電腦周邊、3C、車用、醫療、網通等產品領域。 Founded in 2014, we provide EMS manufacturing services across peripherals, consumer electronics, automotive, medical, and networking products.

公司概況 Company Overview

  • 成立時間 2014 / Established 2014
  • 廠區分佈 三重、桃園 / Sites: Sanchong, Taoyuan
  • 代工領域 電腦周邊、3C、車用、醫療、網通 / Domains: peripherals, 3C, automotive, medical, networking

一句話定位 Positioning

以「少量多樣、快速交付、品質可追溯」為核心，提供從備料、製程、檢驗到出貨的一站式 EMS 服務。 We focus on high-mix low-volume, fast turnaround, and traceable quality—covering material preparation, production, inspection, and shipment.

服務項目 Services

SMT / DIP / Assembly / Testing / Packing / BGA Rework 等服務（可依產品需求彈性調整）。 SMT, DIP, Assembly, Testing, Packing, and BGA rework—flexible to your product requirements.

SMT Surface Mount

印刷、貼片、迴焊、AOI、裁板、目檢與測試銜接。Printing, placement, reflow, AOI, depaneling, inspection.

DIP Through-hole

前後加工、波峰焊、QC / FQC 目檢與出貨。Wave soldering with pre/post processing and final inspection.

組裝 / 測試 / 包裝 Build-Test-Pack

組裝、功能測試、包裝打帶、棧板出貨。Assembly, functional testing, packing/strapping, pallet shipment.
